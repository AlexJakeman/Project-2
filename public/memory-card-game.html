How To Play

This fun game will test your memory as well as help you to improve the way you remember images and their positions in the grid!

  1. 1. The objective is to collect the most pairs of cards.
  2. 2. On each turn, the player can turn over any two cards.
  3. 3. The cards will stay turned over if they're a match.
  4. 4. When a player turns over two cards that do not match, those cards are turned face down again.
  5. 5. The score will increase by 1 every turn.
  6. 6. Lowest score wins.
  7. Good luck!
